Hand cricket is a simple game that can be played by two people. Here are the rules:

  • Overs: Choose how many Overs you want to play - 5 Overs/10 Overs/15 Overs.
  • Total no of Wickets: You will get 7 wickets for this game.
  • Toss: The player to toss chooses either heads or tails,if player wins the toss, they choose batting or bowling.
  • Batting: The batsman throws a number from 1 to 6, if the number they thrown is different from the number the computer throws, they score that number of runs.
  • Bowling: The bowler throws a number from 1 to 6, if the number thrown is the same as the number the batsman throws, the batsman is out.
  • Out: A player is out if they throw the same number as the other player.
